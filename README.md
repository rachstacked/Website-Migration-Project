

# Website Migration Project (Project Management Portfolio)

##  Overview
This project demonstrates end-to-end project management of a website migration from a legacy platform to a modern, scalable solution. The focus was on ensuring improved performance, seamless user experience, and minimal downtime during transition.

---

##  Business Problem
The existing website faced performance issues, limited scalability, and inefficient content management, impacting user experience and business responsiveness. A structured migration was required to modernize the platform while ensuring data integrity and continuity.

---

## Approach
A **Hybrid Project Management approach** was used:
- **Structured planning** for scope, timelines, and stakeholder alignment  
- **Iterative execution (sprints)** for phased migration, testing, and issue resolution  

This ensured both predictability and flexibility during execution.

---

##  Tools Used
- Asana (Gnatt Chart, task tracking & coordination)  
- Google Sheets (Risk Register, KPI Tracking)
- JIRA
- Google docs [Markdown] (documentation & reporting)  

---

##  Project Duration
**6–8 weeks**

- Week 1 → Initiation & Planning  
- Week 2–5 → Execution (phased migration)  
- Week 6 → Testing & Go-live  
- Week 7–8 → Stabilization & Closure

## My Role
- Defined project scope, timelines, and deliverables  
- Coordinated cross-functional teams (Dev, QA, Content)  
- Managed risks, issues, and stakeholder communication  
- Tracked progress through KPIs and status reporting  
- Ensured smooth execution and successful project closure  

---

##  Key Challenges & Solutions

- **Content inconsistencies delayed migration**  
  → Re-prioritized critical pages and coordinated parallel fixes  

- **Risk of downtime during deployment**  
  → Implemented phased migration with backup strategy  

- **Stakeholder alignment gaps**  
  → Introduced structured weekly status reporting  

- **QA issues impacting timelines**  
  → Maintained issue log and prioritized critical fixes  

---

##  Key Outcomes

- Successful migration with **minimal downtime**  
- Improved **website performance and load time**  
- No critical data loss during migration  
- Structured issue resolution and stakeholder alignment  

---

##  Key Learnings

- Early scope clarity prevents rework  
- Proactive risk management is critical  
- Iterative execution improves quality  
- Strong communication drives alignment
