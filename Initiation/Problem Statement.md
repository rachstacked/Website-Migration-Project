## **Problem Statement**

---

The current website is hosted on a legacy platform that presents limitations in performance, scalability, and content management. Users experience slow page load times, inconsistent functionality across devices, and limited accessibility features, which negatively impact user experience and engagement.
From a business perspective, the existing system restricts the marketing and content teams from making timely updates, affecting responsiveness to market needs and SEO performance. Additionally, there are risks associated with outdated infrastructure, including security vulnerabilities and lack of support for modern integrations.
Therefore, a structured website migration is required to transition to a modern, scalable platform that improves performance, enhances user experience, ensures data integrity, and enables efficient content management—while minimizing downtime and operational disruption.
