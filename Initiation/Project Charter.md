##  **Project Name**

**Website Migration to Cloud Infrastructure**

---

##  **Project Description**

This project involves migrating a company’s existing website from a legacy hosting environment to a modern cloud-based infrastructure. The objective is to improve website performance, scalability, security, and reliability while minimizing downtime and ensuring zero data loss during the transition.

---

## 🎯 **Project Goals**

* Improve website load speed by **30–40%**
* Reduce risk of downtime during operations and deployment
* Enhance website scalability to handle increased traffic
* Strengthen data security and infrastructure reliability
* Ensure seamless migration with **zero data loss**

---

## **Project Deliverables**

* Project Charter

* Post-Migration Performance Report

---

##  **Business Case (Cost-Benefit Analysis)**

### 💸 Costs

* Cloud hosting subscription costs
* Migration effort (developer + DevOps time)
* Testing and validation resources
* Temporary operational overhead during migration

---

###  Benefits

* Reduced infrastructure maintenance costs (long-term)
* Improved website performance leading to better user experience
* Increased uptime and reliability
* Enhanced scalability for business growth
* Reduced risk of critical failures and outages

---

###  Net Value

While short-term costs increase due to migration effort, the long-term benefits include **operational efficiency, reduced downtime losses, and improved customer experience**, leading to overall business growth.

## 🎯 **Success Expectations**

### ✅ Acceptable Outcomes

* Website migration completed with **minimal downtime (<30 minutes)**
* No data loss during migration
* All core functionalities working post-deployment
* Improved website performance and load time
* Successful testing and validation

---

### ❌ Not Acceptable

* Data loss during migration
* Extended downtime affecting users
* Broken functionalities after deployment
* Security vulnerabilities introduced
* Lack of rollback or recovery plan
