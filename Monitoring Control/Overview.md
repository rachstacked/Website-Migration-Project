### Monitoring & Control Approach

----

The project progress is continuously monitored using key tracking tools including an Issue Log, Risk Register, and KPI Dashboard.

Issues are logged, prioritized, and tracked to resolution to ensure minimal disruption to project timelines. Risks are periodically reviewed and updated based on project progress.

Key performance indicators (KPIs) are used to measure progress, quality, and overall project health. Weekly status reports and regular stakeholder communication ensure transparency and timely decision-making.
