
## Lessons Learned

### 1. Importance of Clear Scope Definition
Clearly defining in-scope and out-of-scope items early helps prevent confusion and scope creep during execution.

### 2. Early Risk Identification is Critical
Identifying risks such as downtime and data loss early allowed better mitigation planning and reduced impact during execution.

### 3. Continuous Stakeholder Communication
Regular updates and structured communication helped maintain alignment and avoid last-minute surprises.

### 4. Iterative Execution Improves Quality
Breaking the migration into smaller phases (sprints) enabled early issue detection and faster resolution.

### 5. Cross-Team Coordination is Key
Close collaboration between development, QA, and content teams ensured smoother execution and faster issue resolution.
